Account Info

Amazon.com has a very thourough accounts page. After the user logs in, a simple mouse over the tab marked account will drop down a menu with a lengthy list of options along with items recently purchased in case you want to buy them again. Amazon.com targets online shoppers of all kinds and people looking for bargains. Even though their site includes a tremendous amount of information, it is all very well organized using drop downs, which make it easy for the user to navigate.
